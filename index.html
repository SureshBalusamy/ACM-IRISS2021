About IRISS ACM India Annual Event - IRISS 2021

Co-located with the ACM India Annual Event, IRISS is a premier ACM India conference spanning two days. The program features - research scholars including the winner(s) of ACM India best doctoral dissertation award talking about their published work, - eminent early career researchers from academia and industry sharing their experiences, perceptions and ground realities of PhD, and the professional life thereafter, - selected organizations and institutions demonstrating their research credentials and recruitment, career development strategies. The audience comprises students, faculty and industry leaders from across India coming together in an interactive environment, such as panel discussions, informal "Ask me Anything" sessions with the IRISS speakers as well as Annual Event invitees like Turing Award laureates.

The college location lays on Avinashi main road and it is well connected from Airport, Railway station and Gandhipuram town bus stand.
